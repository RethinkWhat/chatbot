How can I open an account with your bank?
You have two convenient options to open an account: visit any of our branches and speak with a representative who will guide you through the process, or complete an online application through our website, which allows you to open an account from the comfort of your home.
What documents do I need to provide to open an account?
To open an account, you'll need to provide a valid form of identification, such as a driver's license or passport, proof of your current address, such as a utility bill or lease agreement, and your Social Security number.
What are the fees associated with opening an account?
We're pleased to offer account opening with no fees associated, making it easy and affordable to start banking with us.
How can I check my account balance?
You can conveniently check your account balance anytime by logging into your online banking account, where you'll find up-to-date information, or by calling our customer service line, where a representative will be happy to assist you.
How can I transfer money between my accounts?
Transferring money between your accounts is simple and can be done through your online banking account by following the easy transfer instructions or by calling our customer service line, where a representative will guide you through the process.
How can I send money to someone else?
Sending money to others is convenient and can be done through your online banking account, where you can initiate a transfer, or by calling our customer service line for assistance.
What are the fees associated with sending money?
There are no fees for sending money to another account within our bank, allowing you to transfer funds easily and free of charge. However, a fee may apply for sending money to an account at a different bank.
How can I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, don't worry! Simply click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, and you'll be guided through the steps to reset your password and regain access to your account.
What should I do if I think my account has been hacked?
If you suspect that your account has been hacked, it's crucial to contact our customer service line immediately. Our representatives are available 24/7 to assist you and take necessary steps to secure your account and protect your funds.
How can I close my account?
You can close your account by visiting any of our branches and speaking with a representative or by calling our customer service line. We'll guide you through the process and ensure a smooth account closure.
What happens to my money when I close my account?
When you close your account, we'll ensure that your remaining balance is transferred to the account of your choice, providing you with control and flexibility over your funds.
How can I dispute a transaction on my account?
To dispute a transaction, log into your online banking account and navigate to the dispute section, where you can submit a dispute form. Alternatively, you can call our customer service line, and a representative will assist you in initiating the dispute process.
What are the fees associated with disputing a transaction?
We don't charge any fees for disputing a transaction, as we want to make it easy for you to address any concerns you may have about your account activity.
How long does it take to resolve a dispute?

What should I do if I am not satisfied with the resolution of my dispute?

How can I protect my account from fraud?
